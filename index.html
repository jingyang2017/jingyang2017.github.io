Biography

Jing Yang is a researcher in the filed of computer vision. Her current research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision and Machine Learning. She has achived the Ph.D. at Computer Vision Laboratory in the University of Nottingham, under the supervision of Georgios (Yorgos) Tzimiropoulos.

More: Google Scholar, Github.
